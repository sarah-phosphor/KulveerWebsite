How I Invest

Two necessary conditions.

High output founders. Deep customer obsession.

Speed is the signal.

300+ investments. The strongest predictor of success isn't valuation or total raised. It's how fast a founder closes their round and gets back to work.

It's not a formula.

I've been around long enough to feel it. Strong founders have an energy you recognize immediately. I wish it were more scientific. It's not.
